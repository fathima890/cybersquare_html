Cybersquare learning platform

Apart from these, they will also get notifications on upcoming live classes,news,and event so that they won't miss them.

Teachers can also login into cybersquare platform and able to vie the students performance, batch progress,make reports ,evaluate their works such as assignments,projects,questions,etc.Teachers can schedule an online class and online exam also.
